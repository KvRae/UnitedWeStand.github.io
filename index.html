Quick overview about our organization

Vision

We recognize that Racism affects all people irrespective of gender, age, language, educational levels, geographic location, class position, religious belief or political affiliation, racial supremacy is the cardinal enemy we must defeat to be able to breathe. We come together now to lead our people to avoid any other false solution to racism.

Mission

Our main goal is to ensure that our environments are free from racial discrimination and harassment, not only but also to take proactive measures to monitor and prevent their occurrence.

Values

One of the best practices to eliminating racial discrimination is learning to recognize and understand your own privilege. Racial privilege plays out across social, political, economic, and cultural environments
